The Prompt
Create a series of three book covers for books that are related. Each design must have unique attributes while also carrying a common theme. Each book cover must include a front and a back, as well as a spine, a UPC code, and the logo of the publisher. This all can be done in any combination of Adobe Photoshop and Adobe Illustrator.
